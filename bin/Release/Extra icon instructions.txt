Using ResHacker:

1. File->Open...->"InKeyKeyboardCreator.exe"
2. Open "Icon Group"
3. Click + and right-click on item.  Choose "Rename Resource"
4. Enter new resource name: 100
5. Action->"Add a new resource..."
6. Click "Open file with new resource..."
7. Find "inkeykeyboardcreatorblank.ico"
8. Give it the resource name: 101
9. File->Save
